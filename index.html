About M&N Cybernetics

We are an early-stage robotics startup focused on building privacy-first home AI systems. Our goal is to design intelligent companions that operate locally, ensuring that user data remains secure, private, and fully under user control.

Our flagship project, Faiz Robot, is designed to assist users with digital tasks, communication, and daily productivity — without relying on centralized cloud data processing.
